Designed and built a self-operating boat capable of collecting floating waste from rivers. Constructed using polyfoam for buoyancy, Arduino-based control system for autonomous navigation, and integrated sensors for obstacle avoidance. Combines mechanical design, electronics, and programming for sustainable engineering.

HW_02
High-Wing RC Plane — NDBFC at GIKI
Designed and built a high-wing RC plane for the National Design Build and Fly Competition at GIKI. Frame constructed with Depron foam board, powered by a 1400kV brushless motor and 2800mAh LiPo battery. Achieved 200g payload capacity and 12-minute flight time.

Autonomous Balloon Popping Robot
Built an autonomous robot using a 2-DOF manipulator to detect and pop red balloons. Integrated an ultrasonic sensor for obstacle avoidance and a color sensor for balloon detection.

Sumo Robot — AI InnoFest 2025
Built a sumo robot from scratch — custom metal chassis, 2 DC planetary gear motors with bevel gear drive system, dual IBT-2 H-bridge motor drivers, and Arduino Nano control with Flysky RC transmitter. Motor driver failed on event day due to thermal overload — learned real-world lessons in torque, gearing, and thermal management.
